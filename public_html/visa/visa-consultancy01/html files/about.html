5Trainers Visa Consultancy is a government-approved language training and immigration consultancy located in Mohali, Punjab. We specialise in IELTS, PTE, study visa, tourist visa, spouse visa guidance.

Our team focuses on clarity and honesty. When you walk into our office or call us, we begin by understanding your profile – education, work experience, financial planning and long-term goals. Based on this, we suggest realistic countries, courses and immigration options that match your situation.

We support you throughout the journey: from language exam preparation to documentation, application filing and pre-departure guidance. Our aim is to make the complex process of overseas education and migration easier and stress-free.
